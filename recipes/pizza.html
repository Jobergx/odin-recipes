Pizza dough

Description

  • A simple and versatile Italian-style pizza dough that can be chilled or frozen, then shaped and cooked by oven or barbecue

Ingredients

  • 1 kg white bread flour or Tipo '00' flour
  • 1 teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 2 x 7 g dried yeast sachets
  • 1 tablespoon golden caster sugar
  • 4 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil

Method

  1. Sieve the flour/s and salt on to a clean work surface and make a well in the middle.
  2. In a jug, mix the yeast, sugar and oil into 650ml of lukewarm water and leave for a few minutes, then pour into the well.
  3. Using a fork, bring the flour in gradually from the sides and swirl it into the liquid.
  4. Keep mixing, drawing larger amounts of flour in, and when it all starts to come together,
  5. work the rest of the flour in with your clean, flour-dusted hands. Knead until you have a smooth, springy dough.
  6. Place the ball of dough in a large flour-dusted bowl and flour the top of it.
  7. Cover the bowl with a damp cloth and place in a warm room for about an hour until the dough has doubled in size.
  8. Now remove the dough to a flour-dusted surface and knead it around a bit to push the air out with your hands
  9. You can either use it immediately, or keep it, wrapped in clingfilm, in the fridge (or freezer) until required.
